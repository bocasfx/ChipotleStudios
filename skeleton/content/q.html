The name of this application is Q, yes... just Q.

Q is a soft MIDI controller that uses particle streams and note nodes to generate MIDI messages. When a particle comes in contact with a node it generates a MIDI message depending on the node's activation probability. The MIDI note's velocity is given by the particle's speed when it collides with a node.

Development platforms: Python, Kivy, Objective-C, Cocos2D.

Sorry, Q is still a work in progress and it's not available for download at this moment.
