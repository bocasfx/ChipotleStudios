My name is Rodolfo Palacios. Husband, dad, musician, cook, software developer. I was born in Mexico City in 1975 but Canada has been my home since 2005.

For many years I struggled deciding wether to be a musician, a software developer or a cook. I have now decided to be all three. Chipotle Studios is my first attempt at that.

I would definitely not describe myself as a competitive person. Instead, I consider myself to be a collaborative person. I understand that my well-being depends not only on me, but on the collective well-being of everyone else around me.
